Pepsi Smart Can

PepsiCo is redefining the beverage experience with Pepsi Smart Cans, a futuristic, digital-first evolution of the classic drink. To bring this innovation to life, Please Call Me Champ Inc was tapped to craft the Boot Up & Power Down motion sequences for the Pepsi Digi Brand Portal - a dynamic, cylindrival interface where content rotates in full 360-degree motion.

I was brought on to animate the graphics that would appear throughout the boot-up and power-down sequences, and to create some procedural animations for when the can would go into sleep mode.

Quick Cube

Quick Cube is an ffx file for After Effects that turns a shape layer into an isometric cube with 3 axes of rotation.

Quick Cylinder

Quick Cylinder is an ffx file for After Effects that turns a shape layer into an isometric cylinder with 2 axes of rotation, and a donut slider.

Download Quick Cylinder
